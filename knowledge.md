DEACOMART AGRIBUSINESS DIGITAL MARKETPLACE PLATFORM
Project Title
Deacomart Digital Marketplace & Agribusiness Management Platform

Motto
"Be EcoWise"

Project Overview
Design and develop a modern, scalable, mobile-first Agribusiness Marketplace and Farmer Empowerment Platform for Deacomart Ltd, Rwanda.

The platform shall serve as a national digital ecosystem connecting farmers, consumers, supermarkets, hotels, restaurants, institutions, agricultural organizations, and consultancy clients.

The system should support:

Direct farmer-to-buyer agricultural trading

Food and beverage product distribution

Farmer training and capacity building

Agricultural consultancy services

Value addition promotion

Food safety awareness

WhatsApp commerce integration

Market linkage services

Digital transformation of Rwanda's agricultural value chain

The platform's primary objective is to eliminate unnecessary middlemen, improve farmer incomes, reduce post-harvest losses, increase market access, strengthen food safety standards, and provide a transparent agricultural marketplace across all districts of Rwanda.
